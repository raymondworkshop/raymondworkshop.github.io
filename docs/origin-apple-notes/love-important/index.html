love important

#love #important a healthy relationship is a partnership between two people who are able to fulfill their own basic needs and stand on their own two feet, with or without a partner

the difference between need and want is the difference between codependence and love loving someone is much more beautiful and rewarding than leaning on them for all things at all times

A need is something you can't live without a want is something you'd like to have but can compromise on

Wanting to be around because she makes you smile, make you happier, and time goes by quicker you don't need them to be, you just want them to be, because she makes you better you want her there because life is more fun with them by your side you want them because you are a complete person without them, but you know that they make you happy

Women need men to show kindness, patience, understanding, empathy, and compassion and consider each other's feelings
