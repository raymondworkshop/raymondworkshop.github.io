english softskills

#english #softskills

how to ask right questions?
- open-end question - how, why, what ?

talk about imaginary situations - If you could go anywhere in the world, where would you go?
- what would u do if you had a million dollars?
- why was that?

learn how to truly listen

share the feelings or emotions when we talk - ecstatic, terrified

#softskills #relationships make friends - relate with each others

small talks - > positive vibes, note negative vibes - strangers, or we don't know very well

the weather - it's so sunny, I love - can you believe ...

physical location - observation + question - this park is so beautifyl, do u come here often?
- is there something you like here that's really great, and you recommend?

personal appearance & clothing - I like your earrings or I like your shoes - I think you have great style - I see your suite/bag, I also ...

avoid religion, politics, money, this is for closer relationship with
