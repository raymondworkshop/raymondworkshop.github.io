Technical Officer – Department of Computing  (Ref: TO (RGC/IDS14/16) 2018-03-23)

Responsibilities

 
•   assist in software development and maintenance in support of the Research Project.

Applicants Should

 
•   possess a relevant bachelor’s degree or above in IT, Computer Science or relevant discipline with at least 6 years of relevant experience;
  • have extensive experience with at least one programming language, e.g., Java, Python, or C++;
  • have relevant experience in most of the following areas:
    • building responsive UI;
    • programming, software development and maintenance;
    • tender preparation and evaluation;
    • preferred skills with innovative techniques, such as cloud computing, big data analytics, and Deep Learning technologies (if not, opportunity will be provided to learn);
  • have good experience in planning, security, project management and documentation;
  • be mature, self-motivated, able to work independently and willing to work on shift;
  • have excellent analytical and problem-solving skills; and
  • have a customer-oriented mindset with good interpersonal and communication skills.

Candidates with less experience will be considered for the post of Assistant Technical Officer.
