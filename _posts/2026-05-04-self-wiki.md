#### Your 'Self-Wiki' 
"Know thyself" is so hard for oneself, while most of time we rely on the feedbacks around people. Now we can use a AI agent for it, based on personal diaries and notes.

The agent incrementally builds and maintains a persistent wiki. When a new diary or note or anything others like your queries, the agent will extract the key information, and integrates these into the existing wiki so that a structured picture of oneself is given over time.  The agent then could be a 'self-awareness engine' for psychological integration.  

The 'Self-Wiki' also can be used on coaching service.
